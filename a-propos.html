Tom Searle

Guitarist Tom Searle was born in England on 23 October, 1987. He died at the age of 28 on 20 August, 2016. Guitarist known for being one of the founding members of metalcore band Band Architects. Their fifth album, Daybreaker, was their first to enter the top 50 of the UK charts.

Dan Searle

Drummer Dan Searle was born in England on 23 October, 1987. He is now 35 years old. Drummer for British metalcore band Band Architects, who co-founded the band in 2004 with his twin brother Guitarist Tom Searle. The band's 2014 album Lost Forever // Lost Together topped the album chart in the US while charting in the UK and five other countries.
